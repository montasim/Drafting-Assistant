# Release the MVP as a free BYOK product

The initial extension will be free to use with no advertisements, account system, paywall, affiliate tracking, or developer-funded Gemini usage. Users supply their own Provider Credential and are responsible for the quota, data handling, and any charges associated with their Google account; any future monetization or paid-model mode must be introduced as a new architectural decision because it would alter the current backend-free trust model.
